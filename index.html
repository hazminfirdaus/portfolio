About Me

I’m Hazmin, a Full-Stack Developer based in Prague with a strong interest in frontend development. I began my career as a Civil Engineer in Malaysia, but after attending a coding bootcamp in 2020, I fell in love with programming and decided to fully shift into tech. That journey led me to complete a Computer Science degree, where I graduated with First Class Honours.

I’ve since built and deployed full-stack applications using JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, PostgreSQL, and Laravel. I especially enjoy creating clean, accessible interfaces but I’m equally comfortable working with backend systems and databases.I’m currently preparing for the next step in my development career and open to opportunities in both frontend and full-stack roles.

When I’m not coding, you can find me playing video games, reading or exploring the outdoors. I’m also a big fan of music and enjoy attending live concerts whenever I can.
